Who We Are

Federation of Procurement and
Student Association (FPSSA)

The Federation of Procurement and Supply Students' Associations (FPSSA) Tanzania was established in 2017 as a visionary, unifying national body dedicated to shaping the future of the procurement and supply chain profession from the grassroots level. Born from the recognized need to consolidate student voices and efforts, FPSSA was founded to provide a robust and structured platform for practical implementation.

FPSSA operates as a legally recognized corporate body with perpetual succession. Our core mission is to empower the next generation of procurement professionals by facilitating students' acceptance of responsibilities, enabling them to embody and practice the core values, ethics, and good conduct essential to the profession.

Our Core Services

Empowering students through development, advocacy, and community.

Professional Skills &
Career Development

We empower the next generation of procurement leaders by providing hands-on training and specialized workshops. Our programs bridge classroom theory with real-world practice, equipping students with technical skills.

National Student &
Industry Advocacy

FPSSA serves as the unified national voice for procurement students. We actively represent student interests to professional boards and industry leaders, ensuring the future of the profession is shaped by student input.

Networking &
Community Building

We build powerful connections among students and industry stakeholders. By facilitating mentorship and collaborative events, we create a supportive community that promotes solidarity and lifelong professional networks.
