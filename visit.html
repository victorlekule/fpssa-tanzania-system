Industrial Visit Report

Tanzania Breweries Ltd

Date

Feb 10, 2024

Attendees

45 Students

Focus

Logistics

Detailed Overview

The Procurement and Supply Chain Management Organization (PSSO) organized an industrial visit to Tanzania Breweries Limited (TBL) Mwanza plant. The primary objective was to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application in the field of supply chain and logistics.

During the visit, students were exposed to the intricate details of:
• Raw material sourcing and quality control.
• Inventory management systems used in large-scale manufacturing.
• The automated bottling and packaging process.
• Distribution logistics across the Lake Zone.

Key Takeaways

  • Understanding the importance of JIT (Just-In-Time) inventory.
  • Safety protocols in industrial warehousing.
  • The role of sustainability in modern supply chains.

Note: Certificates of participation for this tour are available at the club office.

Field Study Report

Mwanza Port Authority

Event Summary

This tour focused on the marine logistics sector. Students toured the Lake Victoria terminal to understand how goods are received, inspected, and cleared for entry into the country.

The Port Manager gave a detailed lecture on:
• Customs documentation (T1, T10 forms).
• Container yard management.
• Challenges in marine transport logistics.

Highlight: Students got to see the loading of the MV Mwanza.
