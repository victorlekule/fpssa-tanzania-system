Transforming Academic Theory into Professional Readiness

Visionary Origins

Established in 2017, FPSSA is a unifying national body dedicated to shaping the future of the procurement profession from the grassroots level. Born from the need to consolidate student voices, we provide a robust platform for discussing procurement issues at regional, national, and international levels.

Legal & Advisory Role

FPSSA operates as a legally recognized corporate body with perpetual succession. We serve as a critical advisory partner to the PSPTB, offering grassroots insights on curriculum and professional development, directly influencing the standards of the field.

Our Commitment to Solidarity

We are passionately committed to fostering volunteerism, self-reliance, and a spirit of solidarity among students, transcending differences in race, culture, and background. Through targeted trainings and networking, we open doors to opportunity while ensuring all procurement clubs align with professional goals.

Core Values

Our Purpose & Direction

Our Mission

To unify, empower, and professionally equip procurement and supplies students through a robust platform for practical learning, ethical practice, networking, and advocacy, while fostering solidarity and advising on professional standards.

Our Vision

To be the premier, influential federation that cultivates ethically grounded, skilled, and innovative procurement and supply chain leaders who drive sustainable development and professional excellence in Tanzania and beyond.
