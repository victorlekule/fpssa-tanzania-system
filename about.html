Who We Are and What Drives Us

The Federation of Procurement and Supplies Student Association

The FPSSA is the premier student-led organization dedicated to bridging the gap between academic theory and professional practice in the vital fields of procurement and supply chain management. Founded by passionate students and supported by industry leaders, we strive to elevate the standard of future professionals.

We host regular workshops, networking events, and mentorship programs designed to provide members with real-world skills and connections necessary to excel in a rapidly evolving global market. Joining FPSSA means committing to excellence and continuous professional growth.
