Our Story

Who We Are and What Drives Us

The Federation of Procurement and Supplies Student Association

The Federation of Procurement and Supply Students' Associations (FPSSA) Tanzania was established in 2017 as a visionary, unifying national body dedicated to shaping the future of the procurement and supply chain profession from the grassroots level. Born from the recognized need to consolidate student voices and efforts, FPSSA was founded to provide a robust and structured platform for the practical implementation and discussion of procurement issues at regional, zonal, national, and international levels. Our establishment marked a pivotal step in transforming academic theory into professional readiness, creating a dynamic bridge between classroom learning and the complex realities of the global supply chain landscape.

FPSSA operates as a legally recognized corporate body with perpetual succession, endowed with the capacity to sue and be sued, borrow funds, and acquire property, thus ensuring its sustainability and operational effectiveness. Our core mission is to empower the next generation of procurement professionals by facilitating students' acceptance of responsibilities, enabling them to embody and practice the core values, ethics, and good conduct essential to the profession. We serve as a critical advisory partner to the Procurement and Supplies Professional and Technicians Board (PSPTB), offering grassroots insights on matters relating to curriculum and professional development, thereby directly influencing the standards of the field.

We are passionately committed to fostering volunteerism, self-reliance, and a spirit of solidarity among students, transcending differences in race, culture, economic background, religion, gender, and geography. Through targeted trainings, seminars, and study tours, we provide essential skills development, while our focus on building extensive networks among students and key industry stakeholders opens doors to opportunity and collaboration. Furthermore, FPSSA is dedicated to creating a conducive environment for meaningful student participation in decision-making processes and monitors all procurement-related clubs and associations established under PSPTB to ensure alignment with professional development goals. In all we do, we strive to promote awareness, togetherness, and the highest standards of professional integrity.

Mission

To unify, empower, and professionally equip procurement and supplies students through a robust platform for practical learning, ethical practice, networking, and advocacy, while fostering solidarity and advising on professional standards for a transformative impact on the industry.

Vision

To be the premier, influential federation that cultivates ethically grounded, skilled, and innovative procurement and supply chain leaders who drive sustainable development and professional excellence in Tanzania and beyond.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Upholding the highest standards of honesty and ethical practice in all our activities.

Innovation

Encouraging new ideas and leveraging technology to solve complex supply chain challenges.

Excellence

Striving for the highest quality in education, training, and professional development.

Community

Building a supportive and collaborative network among students and industry professionals.
